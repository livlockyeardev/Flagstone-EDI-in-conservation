Careers in Conservation

Lira Valencia
Wildlife Ranger, London

"It took the pandemic for me to realise how important working with wildlife was to me. If, like me, finances make it difficult, try to volunteer as local as possible to save time and money and seek organisations which can contribute to your travel and/or food."

Chris Packham
Nature TV Presenter, diagnosed with autism

“We are in a fight to save life on Earth. Neurodiverse people have been shaping civilisation. Without people who think differently, the world's problems will not be solved. I don't see a box."

Ollie Olanipekun
Founder, Flock Together - Birdwatching for BIPOC individuals

“Everyone's connection to nature is different, and that's the beauty of it. Everyone should feel connected to nature, and at the moment, a lot of people don't"
